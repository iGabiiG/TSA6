    Lookism

    Park Hyung-Seok is an unattractive loner who wakes up in a different body. Now tall, handsome, and cooler than ever in his new form, Hyung-Seok aims to achieve everything he couldn't before. How far will he go to keep his body... and his secrets?

    Questism

    Suhyeon Kim is a loser—until a mysterious quest system suddenly appears in real life, offering him missions in exchange for rewards. From small favors to brutal beatdowns, Suhyeon is forced to rise through the ranks of school life. But just how far can a gamer go in the real world?

    How to Fight

    Weak and constantly bullied, Yoo Hobin finds an unexpected way out—by uploading fight videos using secret techniques from a mysterious online channel. As his fame grows, so does the danger. Will his fists and fame be enough to survive the fight of his life?

    Study Group

    Gamin Yoon, a delinquent with a brain full of fists, wants one thing: to study seriously. But in a school full of thugs, studying means surviving. Backed by his loyal study group, Gamin battles to rise above violence and ace his way to college.

    Solo Leveling

    In a world where hunters fight monsters, Jinwoo Sung is known as the weakest of all. But after a near-death experience in a hidden dungeon, he gains a unique ability—one that lets him level up in power with no limit. From weakling to the strongest hunter alive, his journey begins.
